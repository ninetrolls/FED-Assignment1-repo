Bang Chan is Korean although he is Australian by nationality. This is due to him being birthed in Seoul, Korea and then moving to Sydney, Australia at a very younng age. He is the oldest son of his family followed by his younger sister named Hannah and younger brother named Lucas. He also has a pet female Cavalier King Charles Spaniel named Berry. Despite his frustration and despair, Bang Chan kept honing his skills as an artist: singing, dancing, rapping, and producing. He took MIDI classes in JYPE with Changbin and Han, leading them to form the producing group 3RACHA in 2017. He played a big part in the creation of Stray Kids as he was responsible for the selection of members in it. He is a strict compassionate leader, and extreme perfectionest and a father figure in Stray Kids with his life motto being "Just Enjoy!".
