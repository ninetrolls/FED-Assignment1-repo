Likes:

  1. The colour black
  2. Playing video games
  3. Sports, mainly swimming
  4. The seasons spring and autumn

More Information
Bang Chan is Korean although he is Australian by nationality. This is due to him being birthed in Seoul, Korea and then moving to Sydney, Australia at a very younng age. He is the oldest son of his family followed by his younger sister named Hannah and younger brother named Lucas. He also has a pet female Cavalier King Charles Spaniel named Berry. He took dance classes including ballet and modern dance at Newtown High School of the Performing Arts. He also enjoyed playing sports and won many swimming awards, as his dad was a swimming instructor. After moving back to South Korea, he graduated from Cheongdam High School. He has spent 7 years as a JYP entertainment trainee after he joined through an audition in 2010. Due to his long training duration, he has made a lot of friends who are also idols. Despite his frustration and despair, Bang Chan kept honing his skills as an artist: singing, dancing, rapping, and producing. He took MIDI classes in JYPE with Changbin and Han, leading them to form the producing group 3RACHA in 2017. He played a big part in the creation of Stray Kids as he was responsible for the selection of members in it. He is a strict compassionate leader, and extreme perfectionest and a father figure in Stray Kids with his life motto being "Just Enjoy!".

Lee Know was born in Gimpo, South Korea, grew up in Daegot-myeon and attended Daegot Elementary School. He is an only child but has 3 feline brothers, Soonie, Doongie and Dori. Lee Know has multipple hobbies including camping, hiking, choreographing and watching movies. He is ambidextrous with a knack for being a little odd, weird, different and being generally unique. He is able to speak Japanese fluently and is able to converse in basic English. When he was in middle school, he watched a dance video and it made him want to take dance classes. He joined Souldance Studio and stayed there for five years, attending Bico Lim's class. He graduated from Gimpo Jeil Technical High School in computer sciences. At some point, he passed an entrance exam at the Chugye University For The Arts with a self choreography, and joined the campus on March 2017. He is currently enrolled at Gukje Cyber University under the Department of Entertainment Studies. Lee Know participated in various dance competitions with his crew "Cupcakes", notably "World of Dance" in 2016. He is however, also notably knows for being a backup dancer for another boy group BTS and even participated in the "Wings Tour" in early 2017.
